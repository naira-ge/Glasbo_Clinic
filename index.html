    3D/4D Ultrasound

    • 3D ultrasound represents an ultrasonographic method of imaging of a fetus or its structures in a three-dimensional way. 3D ultrasound enables to count more precise volumes of various organs or pathological structures. It also allows better visualization of fetal skeleton, to judge some superficial fetal anomalies, e.g ...
    • 4D ultrasound, unlike 3D ultrasound, represents a real-time three-dimensional imaging. That's why the 4D ultrasound enables to see real fetal movements or its structures three-dimensionally.

    Gynecological Ultrasound

    • Ultrasonographic examination of the uterus, ovaries and pelvis using the 2D/3D imaging and Doppler ultrasound.

    Every pregnancy and childbirth is unique and we are support you in your experience.

    We will provide you in every phase of pregnancy from the earliest stages of planning to follow-up care and lactation support.
